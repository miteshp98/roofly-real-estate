What services does Roofly offer?

Roofly provides comprehensive real estate services, including property listings, expert guidance for buying and selling, market insights, and tools to manage your real estate transactions efficiently.

Are the property listings on Roofly verified?

Yes, Roofly ensures all property listings are verified for authenticity. Our team thoroughly reviews and validates the information before making it available to users.

Does Roofly provide assistance with legal documentation?

Yes, we offer expert guidance for preparing and verifying legal documents to ensure smooth and secure transactions for both buyers and sellers.

Can I schedule property visits through Roofly?

Absolutely! Roofly allows you to schedule property visits at your convenience by contacting the respective agents or sellers directly via our platform.

Does Roofly charge a fee for its services?

Roofly is free for users browsing properties. For sellers and agents, we offer premium listing packages at affordable rates to maximize visibility and engagement.

How can I contact customer support for assistance?

You can reach Roofly’s support team via the "Contact Us" section on our website. We are available to assist you with any inquiries or issues you may have.
